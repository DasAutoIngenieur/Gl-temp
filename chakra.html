Chakra Balancing

7 major Chakras are energy centres of the body corresponding to the 7 major endocrine glands of the body. The health of the chakras being in balance is very much essential for the mental and physical and material well being of a person.

A person trained in Reiki is taught how to clear the chakras and bring balance to the body and mind of the person by balancing the chakras.


The seven major chakras correspond to the 7 major endocrine glands. Clearing and unblocking the chakras and balancing them brings harmony to the mind and body of the person receiving the chakra balancing work.


The health of the chakras are impacted by the lifestyle and emotions of the person including the food. In today's lifestyle involving lots of stress and strain the energies and the health of the chakras are affected negatively causing blockages and stress in the chakras. The clearing of these blockages and powering them with the life force energy from the universe the chakras attain healthy conditions and balance.


During the session the healer will clear the chakras and heal them with universal life force energy and also clear other blockages in the energy body so that proper flow of energy happens throughout the physical body and the aura.
